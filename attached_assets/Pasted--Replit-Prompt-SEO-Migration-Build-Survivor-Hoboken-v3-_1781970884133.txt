## 5. Technical SEO (apply to every page)

- **Canonical:** `<link rel="canonical" href="https://survivorhoboken.com{path}/">` — bare domain, trailing slash, self-referencing.
- **robots.txt:** allow crawling, add `Sitemap: https://survivorhoboken.com/sitemap.xml`, block nothing important.
- **JSON-LD:** `Organization` + `LocalBusiness` sitewide; `Service` on `/private-events/` and its children; `FAQPage` on `/rule-book/`; `Person` on the player pages.
- **Open Graph + Twitter cards** on every page (title, description, image, bare-domain URL).
- **Semantic HTML:** one `<h1>` per page, correct heading order, descriptive link text.
- **Images:** descriptive `alt`, lazy-load, WebP/AVIF, explicit width/height, compressed.
- **Internal linking:** crawlable header + footer nav; link the money pages from the homepage; link `/rule-book/` into `/private-events/`. The two hidden pages stay out of all nav (Section 2).
- **Mobile-first & Core Web Vitals:** 75% of traffic is mobile. Pass LCP, CLS, INP. Minimize and defer JS, fast TTFB.
- **HTTPS only, no mixed content. Preserve the existing Google site-verification and Google Analytics.**
